Workflow

How It Works

Seamlessly integrated into your existing workflows, transforming patient encounters into complete documentation in 5 simple steps.

1

Upload or Record

Capture clinical audio live via our secure web application, mobile app, or upload pre-recorded dictations.

2

AI Speech Processing

Our HIPAA-secure speech engine processes the audio file, separating clinician and patient voices.

3

Medical Recognition

Clinical terminologies, medications, dosages, and active diagnoses are extracted and tagged.

4

Note Generation

Structured clinical summaries (SOAP notes, templates) are generated by clinical LLMs.

5

Export & Sync

Review the note, sign off, and sync directly with Epic, Cerner, Athenahealth, or export as secure documents.

Security & Integrity

Enterprise-Grade Security by Design

Healthcare data demands absolute integrity. Pendo Health Voice is built from the ground up to exceed HIPAA standards and protect Patient Health Information (PHI).

  • ✓

    End-to-End Encryption

    All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3 and at rest using AES-256 with enterprise key management.

  • ✓

    Zero-Data Retention Policy Available

    For high-sensitivity environments, configure session streams to immediately wipe audio files post-transcription.

  • ✓

    Signed Business Associate Agreement (BAA)

    We sign BAAs with clinical organizations, outlining our mutual commitments to safeguard sensitive patient records.
